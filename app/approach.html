Engineering

As a quality-oriented company, Itransition sets up and continuously maintains high standards of software engineering.

  • Compliance

    Committed to the safety and security of our facilities and processes, we ensure strict compliance with domestic and international legal regulations, as well as international cybersecurity and coding standards. Our activities are guided by corporate policies that define the ways of managing business, staff, and client relations.

  • Code quality

    Seeking to achieve code readability, maintainability and testability, we utilize SonarQube, CheckStyle, PMD, StyleCop and other code quality inspection tools, also adhering to SQALE source code evaluation and performing manual code reviews.

  • R&D

    Itransition continuously researches emerging innovations, defining their market applicability and capturing potential opportunities for our clients’ revenue growth. With that, we keep improving the way we conduct our day‑to‑day business and enhance our technology skillset.

  • Security

    Itransition takes security measures throughout the SDLC, adhering to security standards, including OWASP, PCI‑DSS, HIPAA, ISO/IEC 27001, and commonly accepted Secure Coding Guidelines, as well as implementing continuous security testing.
