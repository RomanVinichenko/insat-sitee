People-centric: from 25 to 3,000+

Since 1998, Itransition has grown from a team of twenty-five into an established international software house with 3,000 professionals.

Today Itransition is the team of enthusiastic and forward-thinking project managers, IT consultants, business analysts, software architects, developers, QA and maintenance engineers, etc. They are all different, but they have a few things in common—their talent, persistent desire for self-improvement and willingness to contribute to the success of the company and its clients.
